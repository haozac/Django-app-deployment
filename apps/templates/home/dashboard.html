Introduction

After analysis from the Box Office of movie, we found out that Avatar has the highest of production profit and return ratio. Hence, we decided to look into the Avatar movie reviews to discover why the movie can achieve such amount of profit. This dashboard is to showcase all the visualisation results from the Jupyter Notebook "Avatar Movie Review Analysis" under the folder name "Review Analysis". Details comment are available inside the notebook.

Conclusion

From the visualisation above, we can observe Avatar is favourable from many people all around the world due to its genre and effect the movie used. We could also deduct that the director of this movie: James Cameron is the main factor of why this movie can be so success and be the top box office around the world. Further analysis on other aspects such as appearance of actors and filming time of the movie could be done to improve the answers of why Avatar secure its top ranking in box office and have highest profit and return ratio.
